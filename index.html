About

RUBYCIV

This server is all about building connections, trust, status, and building towards your goals! Your goals are what will define your gameplay. What will your story be?

This server has different recipes and progression than normal! Rails are cheaper, the end is closed off, you can only use netherite for crafting shulker boxes, and international trade is made easier with the server's custom currency which is available in the discord server! Build an empire, form alliances, go to war, steal from rivals, do anything as long as it doesn't break the rules! Claiming land works just like real life, enforce it as yours and others will respect it and recognize it.

This server was created because of Skippy (The server owner) playing on other civilization servers with terrible management. So bad in fact that Skippy created this whole new server, with the goal of having near perfect management! Your reports will go through quickly, and your concerns will be heard.

Server Rules

Have Common Sense, and Play Fair.

Leave Your Baggage at Home

This isn't the place to vent or bring heavy negativity. You're welcome to talk about your life, just be mindful of how it affects the atmosphere for everyone around you.

No NSFW

Please keep things appropriate. Suggestive content isn't welcome here, and anything involving minors in that context is an immediate permanent ban with no exceptions.

Play Nice

Be kind to the real people behind the characters. In-game roleplay conflicts are totally fine as long as everyone is playing their characters in good fun. Never push things to the point where someone genuinely feels upset or stressed.

No Hacking

Hacked clients, xray, and any other cheating tools are not allowed. Full-bright is discouraged but won't get you banned. Trying to pass xray off as "just a resource pack" won't fly here either, we all know what it does.

No Discrimination

Racism, homophobia, transphobia, and bigotry of any kind are not welcome here. Your in-game character can still have rivalries with other civilizations, just keep it in the game world.

Griefing

Griefing that serves the story is totally valid! Going to war and destroying enemy structures is part of the game. However, joining just to randomly grief and leave will result in the damage being undone and a ban. Mass destruction (TNT, flint and steel, etc.) is almost always invalid unless there's a clear in-game reason. Not sure? Just DM Skippy first.

Alt Accounts

Alt accounts are welcome for casual or fun use! Just don't use them to gain an advantage that a regular player wouldn't have, like running double farms, avoiding punishment, or anything that gives you an unfair edge. If you're unsure whether something is okay, just ask Skippy.

Treat New Members Well

Welcome newcomers with kindness! Don't attack new players without a good reason. Everyone deserves a great first impression. Be warm by default, and only dial it back if they're rude to you first.

Trust the Admins

If an admin says something happened, it happened. We have logs and don't make things up. The best thing to do is be honest and own it. Don't argue or demand proof. Just be good, it really is that simple.

No Duping

Duplication machines are not allowed and will be removed on sight. Anyone who builds one will be banned. Any duplicated items found on the server will be deleted. Farms are completely fine, dupers are not.

No Public Annoyances

Please don't pillar jump, leave floating trees, ragebait, or build random structures in busy areas. Keep the shared spaces clean and pleasant for everyone.

No Unfair Advantages

If you're able to do something that gives you a leg up that regular players simply can't do without downloading something or bending other rules, that counts as an unfair advantage. Use your best judgment, and when in doubt, ask.

Be Honest with Admins

Joking around is totally fine. Genuine deception, like denying something we have clear evidence of, is not. Just tell the truth and make it easier for everyone.

No Advertising

Feel free to share your own content with the community sometimes! Just don't promote other servers or beg for views. Doing so, especially before you're a trusted member of the community, can get you removed.

You Have Freedom in Every Other Way!

Outside of these rules, this server is yours to play however you want! Build an empire, form alliances, go to war, steal from rivals, or play a lone wanderer. Claiming land works just like real life, enforce it and others will recognize it. Skippy will never touch your gameplay unless a rule is broken. Just be a good person and have fun!
