About

RUBY CIV

This server is all about building connections, trust, status, and building towards your goals! Your goals are what will define your gameplay. What will your story be?

This server is ran with competence and common sense!

Server Rules

Have Common Sense, and Play Fair.

01

Leave Your Baggage at Home

This is not the server to vent in. Talk to your friends about it, don't make everyone else sad. You can still talk about your life, just not in a way that makes everyone else uncomfortable.

02

No NSFW

This includes anything suggestive. Jokes are fine sometimes, but avoid anything at all (including jokes) with minors.

03

Play Nice

Do not be mean or disrespectful to people outside of the game. In-character conflicts are okay as long as you are playing your characters. Do not go overboard in any way that could make people feel stressed out, sad, or mad.

04

No Hacking

No hacking, xraying, anything like that. Full-bright is discouraged but you won't get banned for it. Don't say "but xraying is just a resource pack!" It's still an unfair advantage, you know what you're doing.

05

Don't Be Racist or Anything Obviously

No racism, homophobia, transphobia, zionism, etc. Just don't suck as a person. Your character can still discriminate against people based on what in-game civilization they are part of.

07

Invalid Griefing

Griefing that makes the story go forward is allowed. Joining just to grief and leave forever is invalid and will be undone with a ban. TNT, flint and steel, and mass destruction are almost always invalid unless there's a real war reason. If unsure, just DM Skippy.

08

Invalid Alt Accounts

You can use an alt for fun or insignificant things, that's it. If your alt lets you do something someone without one couldn't (double farms, evading jail, anything unfair) that's invalid. If you think something might be invalid, just DM Skippy and ask.

09

Treat New Members Well

Be nice and welcoming to new members. Do not kill them without a valid reason. Give them a great first impression. Be respectful by default until they show disrespect to you.

10

If We Say We Caught You, We Caught You

We don't ban people without a reason. Even if you have no idea how we could know, we see all and lying won't do you any good. Don't argue, don't say "show me proof." Just be good. That's the motto.

11

No Duping

Duplication machines will be broken on sight. If they were made after this rule was added, the creator will be banned. All duped items will be deleted. Farms are okay, dupers are not.

12

No General Public Annoyances

Don't jump around placing blocks under yourself, leave floating trees, start random pointless arguments, ragebait, or build random pillars where people go often. Keep it clean.

13

No Unfair Advantages

If you can do something that benefits you that other players can't do without downloading something or breaking other rules, that's an unfair advantage. If it's obviously unfair even if it doesn't fit that definition, it still counts.

14

No Lying to Skippy or the Admins

You can jokingly lie when it's obviously a joke. Actually lying about griefing, your mods, or anything else when we have logs showing the truth is a rule violation.

15

No Advertising

You can share your own videos for fun sometimes. Begging for views or promoting other servers is forbidden and could get you banned, especially if you aren't a trusted member.

06

You Have Freedom in Every Other Way!

Other than these rules you can do pretty much whatever you want! Take over a kingdom if you want, steal if you want, punish people for stealing from you if you want! To claim an area it's just like real life, you don't type a command or do magic, you just enforce the area as yours and everyone recognizes it. Maybe put some flags around too if you really want to. Skippy has the last say in everything, and won't interfere with your gameplay unless you break a rule. Just don't be a bad person and don't hack!
